Hello! I have a bachelor’s degree in Microbiology.

I actually do. Which was followed by a Master’s in Anthropology. A self taught designer who was reading comic books before nerd culture was cool, and was working in startups when startups were considered career dead ends. I’ve since worked in most of the creative industry, from animation studios to big name corporates.
